Profile Summary

I am a software engineer who loves to code and build things. With a background in architecture and academic research in 3D printing, I have developed a profound passion in problem solving and implementing reusable algorithms in my daily work. For example, I developed a parametric model in python that customizes the 3D printer's material flow rate to create a porosity at the microscale level. By utilizing mathematical formulas to construct various rate of flow change, a variety of pore geometries can be designed to customize the structure's localized mechanical properties. The research has led to fruition as it was filed for provisional patent and received further research funding to support commercialization efforts in a 3D printing startup. I have since left the startup to pursue a new career in software engineering by attending a web development bootcamp by General Assembly.
